AudioTime is designed to record, display audio events, and measure the relative time of occurrence between two audio events, and determine the dominant frequency of an audio segment.<br/>
<b>Gestures select graph areas:</b><br/>
Tap one finger to mark the interval yellow.<br/>
Drag the yellow interval mark.<br/>
Two finger pinch or squeeze to zoom.<br/>
Drag to pan left and right.<br/>
Tap twice to remove yellow markers.<br/>
